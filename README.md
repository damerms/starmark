### Project Overview
In 2019, I designed the brand identity—logo, business cards, and favicon—and coded the web presence for StarMark Business Solutions, crafting the site from the ground up with a focus on organic visibility. StarMark is a specialized consulting firm providing cost recovery, cost remediation, and savings strategies.

### Key Contributions
* **Branding & Identity:** Designed the comprehensive visual identity for the firm, including the company logo, custom favicons, and professional business card assets.
